Zayan Malik                                                                    
Zain Javadd "Zayn" Malik is an English singer-songwriter. He auditioned as a solo contestant for the
British music competition television series The X Factor in 2010, where he ended up being a part of
five-piece boy band One Direction, which went on to become one of the best-selling boy bands of all time.

Born: 12 January 1993 (age 32 years), Bradford, United Kingdom
